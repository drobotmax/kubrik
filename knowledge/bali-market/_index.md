## Quick Reference

- **Price range (apartments):** $112,000 - $270,000
- **Price range (villas):** $150,000 - $740,000
- **Price range (townhouses):** $185,000 - $330,000
- **Typical leasehold:** 25-35 years + extension option
- **Projected ROI:** 9.5-20% annually (developer claims)
- **Key locations:** Canggu, Uluwatu/Bukit, Nusa Dua, Ubud, Nuanu
- **Hot trend Q1 2026:** Airbnb licensing crackdown (deadline March 31, 2026)
