## Payment Terms (Common Patterns)

- **Initial deposit:** $3,000-$5,000 (non-refundable)
- **First payment:** 25-50% of total price
- **Installment plans:** Quarterly over 9-12 months, 0% interest
- **Currency:** USD or IDR (some developers offer EUR pricing)
- **Agent commission:** 5-8% (Surfside: 6% base, 7-8% for volume; Lyvin: 5% + $10K bonus)

## Key Economic Insights

1. **Price per m2:** Ranges from ~$3,000/m2 (budget apartments) to ~$6,000/m2 (premium villas)
2. **Leasehold sweet spot:** 30+30 years provides best value/risk balance
3. **Freehold premium:** ~30-50% higher than leasehold equivalent (see BIOM: $350K LH vs $450K FH for comparable villas)
4. **Maintenance fees:** ~$2/m2/month (Surfside benchmark)
5. **Occupancy factor:** Hotel-managed properties claim 70-85% occupancy
