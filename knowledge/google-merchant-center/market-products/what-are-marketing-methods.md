Marketing methods (previously known as Destinations) refer to how and where your products are shown on Google to potential customers. After your product data is uploaded to the Merchant Center, you can use marketing methods to control how your products can be shown to customers across Google or shared with other Google platforms.

The marketing methods are grouped into 3 broad categories:

*   For online stores: 
    *   [Free listings](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/13889434)
    *   [Shopping ads](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/12500202)
    *   [YouTube store](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/12362804)
    *   [YouTube affiliate](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/14815513)
    *   [Dynamic remarketing](https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/6099158)

*   For physical stores: 
    *   [Free local listings](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/14615117)
    *   [Local inventory ads](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/14615117#localaddons)
    *   [Vehicle ads](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/11189169)

*   Others 
    *   Cloud Retail
    *   Local Cloud Retail

**Note**: Depending on your Merchant Center setup and activated marketing methods, you can view different sets of marketing methods across your Merchant Center.

In different sections across your Merchant Center account, you can view and manage how your products can be shown using different marketing methods. You can also filter the list of products by marketing method in the “All products” tab or “Needs attention” tab.

## [](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/15130232?hl=en)How to enable and disable marketing methods

Some marketing methods, like free listings and Shopping ads are enabled on your account during onboarding. For an overview of Marketing methods in your Merchant Center account, select the “Marketing methods” page under “Marketing” in the left navigation menu. You'll be able to view the implementation status of Shopping ads, free listings, local inventory ads and free local listings.

You can also go to the “Add-ons” page under “Settings” ![Image 1: Tools and setting menu icon [Gear]](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/vyDyoYvlunmkVyVjH9CZTw60xwyZpzKoWuPC) in the left navigation menu in your Merchant Center account, to discover, activate, or deactivate any additional marketing methods available to your account.

## [](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/15130232?hl=en)Choose how you want Google to show your products by selecting marketing methods

When you are creating a new product data source, you can select the marketing methods for the products of that data source, regardless of the type of data source (file, API, autofeed, or manually uploaded products). Products inherit marketing methods from the data source that creates them.

You can also select the marketing methods for the products that you are adding one by one manually in Merchant Center.

After you have selected marketing methods for your products, some marketing methods (for example, Shopping ads) require you to set up campaigns so that your products show on Google. Other marketing methods like free listings will start showing for products without further actions after marketing methods are selected for those products and [product review](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/12158380#next-steps) is done.

**Note**: To enable products for Google Shopping, you will need to set up and enable Google Shopping campaigns in Google Ads.

## [](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/15130232?hl=en)Manage marketing methods for your products

There are 3 ways to modify marketing methods for your products to change how your products are shown on Google.

#### **Method 1: By editing marketing methods in data source settings in Merchant Center**

1.   In your [Merchant Center account](https://merchants.google.com/), select Settings ![Image 2: Tools and setting menu icon [Gear]](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/vyDyoYvlunmkVyVjH9CZTw60xwyZpzKoWuPC) in the left navigation menu.
2.   Select**[Data sources](https://merchants.google.com/mc/products/sources)**.
3.   Under the “Product sources” tab, select a product data source.
4.   Select the **Data source setup** tab and select the pencil icon ![Image 3](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/he1Y8zUXKm1xLnkd58njgv6ToOlTRN02hq0L) next to the “Marketing methods” field.
5.   Select which marketing method you’d like to add or remove for products of a selected data source.
6.   Select **Save**. The products of that data source are now able to be shown on Google according to the saved marketing methods.

#### **Method 2: By including or excluding a marketing method for a particular product**

1.   If you'd like to exclude some marketing method for a particular product, you can use the [excluded destination `[excluded_destination]`](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/6324486) attribute to hide it on a product level. You may also choose to specifically include certain products that would otherwise be excluded based on your data source settings by using the [included destination `[included_destination]`](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/7501026) attribute.

## [](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/15130232?hl=en)Filter and view your marketing methods

### Filter products using the product list

1.   In the left navigation menu of your [Merchant Center account](https://merchants.google.com/), select **Products**.
2.   Under the “All products” tab, select the filter icon ![Image 4](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/KNBfKo2b7Jb49LtVM7NE7nN3F3hEA7gN8UXs) above the table.
3.   Choose **Marketing methods** filter.
4.   Select which marketing method you’d like to filter your products for.
5.   Select **Apply**. You are now able to view the products showing according to the marketing methods.

### Filter products on the “Needs attention” page

1.   In the left navigation menu of your [Merchant Center account](https://merchants.google.com/), select **Products**.
2.   Under the “Needs attention” tab, select the “Marketing methods” filter.
3.   Select which marketing method you’d like to filter your products for.
4.   Select **Apply**. You are now able to view the products showing according to the marketing methods.

### View marketing methods from “Product details” page

1.   In the left navigation menu of your [Merchant Center account](https://merchants.google.com/), select **Products**.
2.   Under the “All products” tab, select a product.
3.   Under the “Product details” tab, you can view the marketing methods that product is intended for.

**Note**: For products added to the Merchant Center manually one by one, you can change marketing methods in the product editor of these products.
