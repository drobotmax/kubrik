## Unable to sign in to your account

If you're having trouble signing in to your Merchant Center account, ensure that you're using the correct email address and password. If you're using the correct username and password, try deleting your browser's cookies and cache before trying to access Merchant Center again. Additionally, enable SSL 2.0 and cookies and confirm that any automatic configuration scripts or proxies that you're using aren't disabling cookies. Learn how to [turn cookies on or off](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/61416).

If you've forgotten your password, follow these steps to [Change or reset your password](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/41078).

[Contact us](https://support.google.com/merchants/contact/howto2?ctx=direct) to change your sign-in email address, as users aren't able to change their sign-in email directly.

**Note**: If you receive an error stating that you don't have access to Merchant Center, check Google Workspace to inspect if you have enabled access. Further, you can contact your company's Google Workspace administrators and ensure that your email ID is enabled for you to access Merchant Center.

## Deleting an account

You can remove the contents of your Google Merchant Center account, but the process differs for standalone accounts and sub-accounts. Review the following sections for the steps to delete your account. You can also permanently delete the account that's associated with the Google Merchant Center. Learn how to [Delete your Google account](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/32046).

### Before you begin

*   Ensure you are signed in with an administrative user account.
*   Download any product data or reports you want to keep. Learn [how to download your product information](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/12490893#download).
*   Cancel any active transactions.

### [](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/15114545?hl=en)How to delete your standalone account

Deleting your standalone account will permanently remove all data and stop your products from showing on Google within 48 hours.

1.   In your [Merchant Center account](http://merchants.google.com/), click Settings ![Image 1: Tools and setting menu icon [Gear]](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/vyDyoYvlunmkVyVjH9CZTw60xwyZpzKoWuPC) in the left navigation menu.
2.   Select **General**.
3.   Under the "Account setup" tab, scroll down and select **Close Merchant Center account**.
4.   Review the disclaimer and select “Yes, I want to permanently close this Merchant Center account”.
5.   Click **Close Merchant Center account**.

### How to delete your sub-accounts

If your account is part of a Multi-Client Account (MCA) or managed by a third party, it must first be converted into a standalone account before it can be deleted.

1.   **Unlink the account**: [Contact Google Support](https://support.google.com/merchants/gethelp) to request that the sub-account be unlinked from its parent account. You will need to provide formal authorization. This process typically takes 24-48 hours.
2.   **Close the account**: Once the account has been converted to a standalone, follow [the steps above](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1HNZsLesX1VIi4SQiWcHs3duWebIbmjuTTUKLETsshjE/edit?tab=t.1pmq0hj136vm#bookmark=id.4xj0oe97h7na) in "[How to delete your standalone account](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/15114545?hl=en#Howtodeleteyourstandaloneaccount)" to delete it.

### Restoring a deleted account

If you accidentally deleted your Merchant Center account, it may be possible to restore it within 30 days. You will need to [contact Google Support](https://support.google.com/merchants/gethelp) and provide formal authorization. Accounts deleted for more than 30 days usually cannot be recovered and will require you to create a new account.

Learn more about [Deleting your Merchant Center account](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/12160374).

Learn more about [Deleting your Google Account](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/32046).
