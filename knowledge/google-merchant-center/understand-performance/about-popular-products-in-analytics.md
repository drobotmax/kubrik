The Popular products tab shows you cards with the most popular products and brands on Google and the current availability for your products. This article explains how you can use those insights to help you understand, optimize, and grow your business with Google.

## [](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/13299535?hl=en)Benefits

When you know what your potential customers are likely to buy, you can make better choices about which products and brands to stock and advertise. Here are ways you can use the Popular products tab:

*   **Check what’s popular on Google**: Check if you carry products that people like on Google. Make sure you have the right items in stock and sell at competitive prices.
*   **Prioritize popular products**: Use the report to help you decide how much to spend on ads for popular products and brands you sell.
*   **Plan ahead for holidays**: Find out what sold well in the past, make ads for those products and brands, and keep track of how they're doing.

## [](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/13299535?hl=en)How it works

You can expect updated Popular products data every week, though the new data included in your metrics may take up to 2 weeks to become available.

To view the popular products, follow the steps below.

![Image 1: Step 1](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/Lqn87u8yoXab3NvtMYC58WwYvAWuz065pCoR) In your [Merchant Center account](https://merchants.google.com/), under the Analytics section, go to ![Image 2: An icon for the Products in Merchant Center.](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/Xse48YyKwuL61aLaftpIVoLnkJcvqeNvh8kx)**Products**.

![Image 3: Step 2](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/sXXE8ayMzITWqMQhoFgSh1dUY1YFF37QlfLJ) Click on the **[Popular products](https://merchants.google.com/mc/reporting/performance/products)** tab. This tab shows a selection of personalized “cards” based on the products that you sell and in which countries. You can find answers to key questions such as:

> *   _Which are the most popular products and brands?_
> *   _Which brands are gaining popularity in the market?_
> *   _Which of the products that you sell are most popular on Google?_

To find out more information and dive deeper into a specific product category or time range, click on the card to open up a more advanced experience that provides in-depth information.

## [](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/13299535?hl=en)What’s available?

### Your popular products on Google

Find out which products you sell are the most popular on Google. Click on the specific category to get a list of all products or assess which ones are most popular in your most relevant categories.

### Your popular brands on Google

Find out which brands you sell are the most popular on Google. Google ranks products based on how often customers are buying products from these brands. The smaller the popularity rank number, the higher your brand ranks in popularity. You can click “View brand details” to receive a list of all popular brands, learn which of their products you currently sell, and get recommendations on other products to consider selling.

### Your products that are out-of-stock

Identify which of your popular products are not showing on Google because they are out-of-stock. You can click “View product details” to explore a list of suggested products to consider restocking in your most relevant categories.

### Consider stocking more products from brands you sell

Find more products from your brands that customers are buying on Google. Click on a brand to explore stocking suggestions of products that customers are buying most on Google. You can click ”View brand details” to see the full list of popular brands.

### Top-selling products on Google

Explore which products consumers are buying in the most relevant categories based on your inventory. You can click on “View product details” to view the full list of product suggestions in a selected category or any other category that you are interested in.

### Brands that are gaining popularity on Google

Learn which brands have experienced the biggest jumps in popularity. You can click on “View brand details” to get the full list of brand details and categories.

**Note**: For your convenience, if Google has identified that you are selling only your own products, we may hide cards with product suggestions. For example, if you’re a business that sells only shoes manufactured by you, we won’t suggest stocking products from other shoe manufacturers.

### Explore insights beyond cards

Click the "View" buttons on each card to explore more detailed information on each topic. Filter or sort your results based on the following dimensions:

*   **Popularity rank:** Google ranks products and brands based on how often customers are searching for them. The smaller the number, the higher your product or brand ranks in popularity. 
    *   **Rank change:** How much a brand has increased in popularity compared to a previous period because of customers buying products from this brand more often.

*   **Availability:** Details whether you have the product marked in Merchant Center as “in stock” or “out-of-stock”. If you don’t sell the product or brand yet, you'll have a suggestion to consider stocking the product or brand. 
    *   **In stock:** Because you set the availability of this product as “In stock”
    *   **Out of stock:** Because you set the availability of this product as “Out-of-stock”
    *   **Product suggestion:** Because this product is popular across Google and you already sell this brand, you might want to consider adding this new product to your inventory.
    *   **Brand suggestion:** Because this brand is popular across Google, and you currently don't sell it, you might want to consider adding this brand to your inventory.

*   **Price range:** What prices are currently like for this product across Google.
*   **Products to consider stocking:** Products you don’t sell that customers are buying from other sellers on Google.
*   **Country**: One of the countries you sell in is selected by default, but you can explore popular products and brands in other countries you do not sell in yet by changing the filter values.
*   **Category**: The most relevant category you sell products in is selected by default, but you can change it to explore other levels of granularity you’re interested in.
*   **Time period**: Select a monthly or weekly time period going back to 2 years to understand product and trends over time.

The report information is available for download. ## **[](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/13299535?hl=en)**Compliance to Merchant Center terms and conditions

The Popular products reports are only available for the internal use of the retailer or those acting on the retailer's behalf. Popular products data cannot be resold, publicly displayed, advertised, or aggregated across businesses. For purposes of Popular products, “retailer” means the business entity owning the domain of the products being sold or selling products on a marketplace.

You must ensure that your use, or any third party's use, of Popular products data exported from your Merchant Center account complies with the [Merchant Center terms and conditions](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/12628444).
