An overview of Search Console's reports and tools

Below is a summary of current Search Console reports and[settings](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9133276?hl=en#Settings), and useful[legacy tools and reports](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9133276?hl=en#additional_tools).

| **Report or tool** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| [**Overview page**](https://search.google.com/search-console) | Shows a summary of your property's health, including any manual actions or security issues applied to your site, graphs of total clicks and error/valid indexed pages, and summaries of error/valid enhancements for your site (AMP pages, structured data found on your site). **Suggested use:** If only checking in periodically, check for manual actions or security issues. Look for spikes in coverage or enhancement errors, and if found, open the report to troubleshoot. Look for dramatic dips in your click count; if found, open the report to investigate. **Useful for:** Everyone |
| [**Insights report**](https://search.google.com/search-console/performance/insights) | A simple overview of your site's performance. The report summarizes key metrics like clicks and impressions, and highlights your top and trending content, search queries, countries, and traffic sources from Google Web Search results and other Google properties. **Suggested use:** Quickly understand what content resonates with your audience and identify opportunities for new or improved content. Use this as a starting point before diving into more granular data in the Performance reports. **Useful for:** Content creators, site owners, and marketers looking for quick insights. |
| **Performance reports:** * [**Web Search**](https://search.google.com/search-console/performance/search-analytics) * [**Discover**](https://search.google.com/search-console/performance/discover)**‡** * [**News**](https://search.google.com/search-console/performance/google-news)**‡** **‡****_Available only if your property has sufficient traffic on Discover or News_** | Explore how many people saw and clicked on your site in Google Search, what queries showed your site in Search, and your average search position. If your site has [Discover data](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9046777) or [News data](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/10083653), you will see an appropriate report. Data includes clicks, impressions, CTR, and position for your site, as well as query strings that users were searching for. Data can be grouped by page URL, country of query, or device type. **Suggested use:** Periodically review to check for spikes or dips in clicks or impressions. Examine the query strings that show your site, and see if the queries that you expect show your site. [Read more about traffic drops on your site.](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9079473) **Useful for:** SEOs, site owners [[_Performance on Search results documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/7576553)] [[_Performance on Discover documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9216516)] [[_Performance on Google News documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/10083653)] |
| [**URL Inspection tool**](https://search.google.com/search-console?action=inspect) | See the information that the Google index has about a specific URL on your site, or test a live page. Information is quite extensive, and includes page HTML code and errors, AMP, JavaScript output and errors encountered when rendering the page, any canonicals selected for a page, and more. Use the tool by typing a URL in the bar at the top of Search Console. **Suggested use:** Use this report to troubleshoot any crawling errors that Google encountered on your site. You can reach this tool by clicking into a specific URL in the Coverage report. You can also use this report to fix and retest a page before resubmitting the page for indexing. **Useful for:** SEOs, developers [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9012289)] |
| [**Page indexing report**](https://search.google.com/search-console/index) | Shows index status for all the pages in your site. Pages are grouped according to whether they are, or can be, indexed, with a description of why they could or could not be indexed. **Suggested use:** Periodically scan for spikes in errors, or drops in indexed counts to identify crawling problems on your site. **Useful for:** SEOs, developers/site administrators [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/7440203)] |
| [Video indexing report](https://search.google.com/search-console/video-index) | This report is generated for sites with video pages (any page containing a video). The video indexing report shows how many videos on your site are eligible for video features on Google Search, and provides reasons why other videos on your site couldn't be indexed. **Suggested use:** Periodically ensure that most of your [watch page videos](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/video?sjid=11461338133288657227-NC#indexing-criteria), and especially your important video pages, have been indexed by Google. If not, use the report to troubleshoot why. **Useful for:** SEOs, site owners. [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9495631)] |
| [**Sitemaps report**](https://search.google.com/search-console/sitemaps) | Shows which sitemaps have been submitted for your site, sitemap statistics, and any errors Google encountered when processing your sitemaps. **Suggested use:** Periodically check to see if Google could process your sitemaps, or use for debugging if Google can't seem to find new pages on your site. **Useful for:** SEOs [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/7451001)] |
| [**Removals tool**](https://search.google.com/search-console/removals) | Allows you to temporarily block URLs on your site from appearing in Google Search, or to clear the search snippet until the next document crawl. Also shows a history of your block requests, as well as a history of user requests to mark pages on your site as adult-only. **Useful for:** Site owners [[Documentation](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9689846)] |
| [**Core Web Vitals report**](https://search.google.com/search-console/core-web-vitals) | Shows how your page performs in real-world usage. Suggested use: Check how well your pages perform for your users; fix poorly performing pages. **Useful for:** SEOs, Developers [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9205520)] |
| [**AMP report**](https://search.google.com/search-console/amp) | See which AMP pages on your site could or couldn't be indexed. **Suggested use:** Periodically check for issues affecting all AMP pages on your site, or to see if you have an unexpected spike in errors or drop in indexed AMP pages. **Useful for:** SEOs, developers [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/7450883)] |
| **Rich Result Status reports** | See which rich results Google could or couldn't crawl. Each [**rich result**](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/7506797) type has a separate report. Only a subset of rich results are supported; see the documentation to learn which types are supported. A specific report type will be present only if Google has detected items of that type on your site. **Suggested use:** Periodically scan for errors and debug the issues. Can also be used to see how many rich results of a given type Google is able to process. **Useful for:** SEOs, developers [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/7552505)] |
| [**Manual actions report**](https://search.google.com/search-console/manual-actions) | Shows any manual actions against your site. **Suggested use:** If you see a manual action in your overview page, open the report to learn details about the report, how to fix the issue, and to file a reconsideration request after you have fixed the issue. **Useful for:** Everyone [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9044175)] |
| [**Security issues report**](https://search.google.com/search-console/security-issues) | Shows any security issues detected by Google on your site. Suggested use: If you see a security issue in your overview page, or if your site is showing browser alerts for dangerous or hacked content, open the report to learn details about the report, how to fix the issue, and to file a reconsideration request after you have fixed the issue. **Useful for:**Everyone [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9044101)] |
| [**Links report**](https://search.google.com/search-console/links) | Shows which sites link to yours, what the link text is, and internal link targets within your own site. **Suggested use:** Can be used to determine if you are linked to by either spammy or useful sites. **Useful for:** SEOs [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9049606)] |
| [**Achievements**](https://www.google.com/url?q=https://search.google.com/search-console/achievements&sa=D&source=docs&ust=1756319672678626&usg=AOvVaw0LOUnHHuUiS8iraxc1hrAe) | Achievements are like milestones for your site, such as reaching your first 1,000 clicks. Monitor the single achievement you’re working toward and the milestones you’ve already reached. **Suggested use:** Use this report to track progress, celebrate milestones, and get ideas to continue growing your content. **Useful for:**Site owners who want an overview of their progress. |
| [**AMP Test tool**](https://search.google.com/test/amp) | Test any AMP page for validity. This functionality is available in the URL Inspection report, but the AMP Test tool works on pages outside a property that you own and includes less information than the URL Inspection report. **Useful for:** Developers, technical SEOs [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/7320015)] |
| [**Rich Results Test tool**](https://search.google.com/test/rich-results) | Test any page for rich results. This functionality is available in the URL Inspection report, but the Rich Results Test tool works on pages outside a property that you own and includes less information than the URL Inspection report. **Useful for:** Developers, technical SEOs [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/7445569)] |

## [](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9133276?hl=en)Settings

These tools and reports can be found on Search Console under **Settings**.

| **Report or tool** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| [**Ownership verification**](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9008080) | Prove to Search Console that you own a specific website. **Suggested use:** Confirm ownership to activate Search Console for your site and get access to your Google Search data. **Useful for**: Site owners [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9008080)] |
| [**Users and Permissions**](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/7687615) | Manage users and permissions for your Search Console property. **Useful for:**Site owners [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/7687615)] |
| [**Associations**](https://search.google.com/search-console/settings/associations) | Manage your property associations to enable data sharing and more between your property and Google Analytics, YouTube channels, Android apps, and other Google services. **Useful for:** Site owners [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/1120006)] |
| [**Change of address**](https://search.google.com/search-console/settings/change-address) | Tell Google when you migrate your site to another domain. **Useful for:** Site owners, site developers [[_Documentation and tool link_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9370220)] |
| [**Bulk data export**](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/12917675) | Export your Search Console performance data to Google BigQuery. This will help you store and analyze the data more effectively. **Suggested use:** Advanced data analysis. **Useful for:** SEOs, Site owners [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/12917675)] |
| [**Shipping and return settings**](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/14907594?hl=en&sjid=8086282397495006184-NC) | Allows online merchants to use Search Console to add, view, and manage shipping and return policies. ![Image 1: tip](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXc10QUugpjejbmQfgj2_8UQQcHqH4augaL0xVuBct2m_P86BBoOnN8TadtlK4BgmVdu7y3C-RTW8G1xajZcGpcjn5NFVz5X4cWJ5XQZr_Q9JQq00XeiPj5qEB6vxS4QUpdkVYVu_UUA8ptLugIqjpwSyeLEg4iCy45MHSEzrRQwadERWHmpdtU-W1sM2J5Z1A?key=fhK2WtXF3m-LrMsOoMROcg) Only available to owners or full users of a Search Console property. If you have a Merchant Center account, you’ll need to [associate it with your Search Console property](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9419894?hl=en&sjid=6935562983160265888-NC#zippy=%2Crequest-an-association). **Suggested use:** Add your site’s policies and periodically check to see if Google could verify their status. Edit any policies that don’t match information on your website. **Useful for:** Merchants that haven't set up shipping/return policies. [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/14907594?hl=en&sjid=8086282397495006184-NC)] |
| [**robots.txt report**](https://search.google.com/search-console/settings/robots-txt) | See whether Google can process your [**robots.txt**](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/12818275) files. **Suggested use:** Good for debugging/fixing blocked resources or pages on your site. **Useful for:** SEOs, developers [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/6062598)] |
| [**Crawl stats**](https://search.google.com/search-console/settings/crawl-stats) | Shows how often Google is crawling your site, and how many requests it is making. **Suggested use:** If your site is being overloaded by Google crawl requests, you can track the issue here. ![Image 2: tip](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeYb51qhvepkP9nsHoqyd0WELzoCITgZaJgSgd1pC7nVfEfDWx3YDP2HckrhG6DyOnpCSmf_UNpdDZWYKddkQyU3_RMn4Iyja-An7dypKDc94J0yaSduZn6403M9mRCOUZrBWPL81iv5Fa_vtOVMPH72UTzAgo4gSUdCaiAQgBqPMHxxwcx3icz2lF6TDvcOw?key=fhK2WtXF3m-LrMsOoMROcg) This report is available only for domain-level properties (example.com, m.example.com, http://example.com) not for URL-prefix properties (example.com/somepath/). **Useful for:** Advanced SEO, website administrators [[_Documentation_](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9679690)] |

## [](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9133276?hl=en)Legacy tools and reports

These tools and reports do not yet have a full replacement in new Search Console, but can still be reached at the links given below. The Search Console team is working on a replacement strategy for these items.

| Report or tool | Description |
| --- | --- |
| [Data Highlighter tool](https://www.google.com/webmasters/data-highlighter/) | A simple tool to help Google extract data from template pages on your site. **Suggested use:** If your site has many pages with templated data (for example, bus routes or event schedules) and you can't use structured data for some reason, you can use this tool to help Google extract similar data from identically coded pages. You must retrain Google every time you change the design of the page. **Useful for:** Site owners on basic site hosting services [_[Documentation](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/2692911)_] |
| [Web Tools](https://www.google.com/webmasters/tools/ad-experience-unverified) | Various other tools that might be useful for your site. |
