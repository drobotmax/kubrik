# Available call-to-action buttons in Meta Ads Manager
Meta Ads Manager lets you add a call-to-action button to your ads to encourage people to take an action that aligns to your campaign goal. The list of call-to-action buttons you can choose from differs for each campaign objective. You can use the [Ads Guide](https://www.facebook.com/business/ads-guide/video) to learn which call-to-action buttons are available for each ad format. 
If you're having issues, learn how to [troubleshoot call-to-action buttons](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/125897810822573) in existing post ads.
