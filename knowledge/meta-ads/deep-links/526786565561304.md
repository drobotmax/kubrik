# Управление разрешениями для партнерской рекламы на уровне аккаунта в Центре партнерской рекламы
Branded content ads are now called partnership ads.
Through [Partnership Ads Hub](https://business.facebook.com/latest/partnership_ads_hub), advertisers can manage partnership ad relationships with their partners at the account-level and run partnership ads without additional permissions at the content-level.
From Partnership Ads Hub, advertisers can send account-level permissions requests to their partners, who can view and accept these permissions in the Instagram app and Facebook app. Advertisers can accept requests they receive from their partners, view pending requests, accepted requests that indicate active partnerships, and inactive requests for which creators or brands have removed permission.
When advertisers send an account-level permissions request, advertisers can see if the creator or partner has linked their Instagram and Facebook accounts. If the partner’s accounts are linked, the partner will receive notifications on both Instagram and Facebook. Accepting the permission request will give advertisers account-level permissions for both accounts.
**Advertisers can** :
  * Create partnership ads from a partner’s handle without pre-existing content.
  * Create partnership ads from any of the partner’s existing posts, stories or reels that tag the advertiser, including archived content. **Note** : To boost Instagram content, the advertiser must be tagged using either the paid partnership label, @mentions, people tags, product tags, or Instagram Collabs. To boost Facebook content, the advertiser must be tagged using the paid partnership label.
  * Include or exclude their partner’s custom audience in their partnership ad campaign.


**Creators and partners can** :
  * Tag the advertiser in the paid partnership label when creating branded content.


### How to send a partnership ad request
You can send partnership ad requests in the Partnership Ads Hub:
  1. In [Ads Manager](https://adsmanager.facebook.com/adsmanager/), click ![](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/mci_ab/public/cms/?ab_b=e&ab_page=CMS&ab_entry=1769423977291307&version=1774271376&transcode_extension=webp)**All tools** in the bottom left corner.
  2. Below **Advertise** , click **Partnership Ads Hub**.
  3. Click **Add partnership** in the top right corner.
  4. Search for the partner’s username, then select their account.
  5. Click **Next**.
  6. Select the account you want to use to partner with the creator, then click **Done**.
  7. Click **Next** , then click **Send request**.


Your partner will be notified and can accept the request in Partnership Ads Hub, the [Instagram app](https://help.instagram.com/742368993017210?helpref=faq_content), or the Facebook app.
Once the partner accepts your request, account-level permissions will be established and you can create partnership ads with this partner without additional permission requests.
**Note** : Partners who have not accepted the request have a **Request sent** status in the **Partners** tab. Select the partner to see more and manage the request. If your partner has an Instagram account, you can click **Get request link** to share a link that takes them directly to the screen where they can view and accept the request in the Instagram app. You can also click **Notify again** to re-send the request notification.
