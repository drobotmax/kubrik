Call Insights in Meta Business Suite helps you track and gain deeper visibility into your calls that you receive through your call ads. It provides a centralized view of all calls generated by your call ads. With Call Insights, you can:
  * **Analyze ad success:** Understand which ads are driving the most valuable calls, so you can focus your budget on what works best and optimize campaigns for better performance.
  * **Filter customer calls:** You can filter your customer calls by ad account ID, campaign ID, ad set ID, ad ID, Page ID, call duration, call quality and dates the calls took place.
  * **Set customized call quality:** Choose a custom duration (number of seconds) for reporting that you consider to be a quality call.


**Note:** Call Insights shows data for calls placed though call ads only. It does not include information on calls that come from Messenger or WhatsApp.
To access Call Insights, go to Meta Business Suite, click **Call Insights** in the left menu or click **All tools** and find it under **Analyze and report**.
## Call Insights metrics
Call Insights offers column presets with key metrics and details about the calls generated from your call ads. These metrics can help you understand how your campaigns support your business goals. Some of the metrics and details available in Call Insights are:
  * **Start time:** The time the call was placed
  * **Call duration:** How long the call lasted
  * **Quality call:** Whether the call meets your selected quality call duration or not
  * **Cost per Quality Call (CPQC):** Average cost per each qualified call that meets your selected duration

### What if I don't see quality call or cost per quality call data?
If you see the **Quality call** column or the **Cost per quality call** metric empty, you will need to define your quality call duration (the minimum duration for which you want to consider the call as a quality call) using the **Quality call** slider at the top right corner of the tool.
