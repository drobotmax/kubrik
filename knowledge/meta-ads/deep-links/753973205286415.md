# Create a call add-on from Meta Ads Manager
Lead ads with calling, such as call add-ons, are designed to give people more time to learn about your products or services on your website before they call you. Call add-ons can include a website as your destination and a call-to-action button, such as **See details** , **Learn more** or **Contact us**.
**Note:** We renamed call extension ads to call add-ons to better describe their functionality.
When you select **Sales** as your ad objective, you’ll need to select Website as your main destination and then edit your personalized destination to include a call add-on. Learn [about personalized destinations](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/1842103166667481).
When someone taps on a call add-on or the call-to-action button, they’ll be redirected to your website within the in-app browser, with a **Call now** button visible at the bottom. They’ll be asked to confirm the call and prompted to call, but can still exit out of the screen.
## Create a call add-on from Meta Ads Manager
![](https://scontent-prg1-1.xx.fbcdn.net/v/t39.8562-6/146597050_455534749152435_1802453867954835002_n.svg?_nc_cat=1&ccb=1-7&_nc_sid=f537c7&_nc_ohc=aXVV8xvJ_GcQ7kNvwHlDc6r&_nc_oc=AdpxRCyIaZgWtCiyYVpouqc9iu5YqUDJMwrDTBJEFNEJZUEPEvWvXOLbI_2mhUNO9Is&_nc_zt=14&_nc_ht=scontent-prg1-1.xx&_nc_gid=gamHxOiIpWIFnLBrw9M_mQ&_nc_ss=7a389&oh=00_Afw_2gG5Y8cGsUf8j60zOenqwhwdFasbLdz6rBbuUDVYmQ&oe=69CF18D8)
### Create a call add-on using the traffic objective
  1. Go to [Ads Manager](https://www.facebook.com/ads/manage/campaigns) and click **+ Create.**
  2. Select **Traffic** as your ad objective, then click **Continue**.
  3. Complete the ad creation steps until you reach the **Conversion** section at the ad set level.
  4. In the **Conversion** section, select **Website** as your conversion location. Then, use the dropdown menus to choose a performance goal and a dataset.
  5. To deliver your ad on specific hours and days so you can receive calls at your convenience, choose to use **Lifetime budget** in the **Budget & schedule** section. Click **Show more settings** , check the box next to **Set a schedule for ads** , and then click and drag to schedule multiple times.
  6. Complete the ad creation steps until you reach the **Ad setup** section at the ad level.
  7. In the **Ad setup** section, select either **Single image or video** , **Carousel** or **Collection**.   
**Примечание.** С марта 2026 года формат подборки больше не будет показываться при **настройке рекламы**. Подборку можно выбрать в **вариантах отображения формата** в разделе **Рекламные креативы**. [Подробнее об использовании вариантов отображения формата.](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/1061244856034746)
  8. Complete the **Ad creative** section.
  9. In the **Destination** section, select **Website** and enter a website URL and display link.
**Note:** Starting in March 2026, all single media and Advantage+ catalog collection ads on Facebook Feed will no longer display a URL in the ad footer. 
  10. In the **Browser add-ons** section, select **Call** and enter a phone number. Change your country code from the dropdown menu, if needed.
  11. In the **Tracking** section, click **Set up** to set up CRM events, website events and app events to track event datasets, if needed.
  12. When you're done, click **Publish** to publish your lead ad.

### Create a call add-on using the leads or sales objective
  1. Go to [Ads Manager](https://www.facebook.com/ads/manage/campaigns) and click **+ Create.**
  2. Select **Leads** or **Sales** as your ad objective, then click **Continue**.
  3. Complete the ad creation steps until you reach the **Conversion** section at the ad set level.
  4. In the **Conversion** section, select **Website** as your conversion location. Then, use the dropdown menus to choose a performance goal and a dataset.
  5. To deliver your ad on specific hours and days so you can receive calls at your convenience, choose to use **Lifetime budget** in the **Budget & schedule** section. Click **Show more settings** , check the box next to **Set a schedule for ads** , and then click and drag to schedule multiple times.
  6. Complete the ad creation steps until you reach the **Ad setup** section at the ad level.
  7. In the **Ad setup** section, select either **Single image or video** , **Carousel** or **Collection**.   
**Примечание.** С марта 2026 года формат подборки больше не будет показываться при **настройке рекламы**. Подборку можно выбрать в **вариантах отображения формата** в разделе **Рекламные креативы**. [Подробнее об использовании вариантов отображения формата.](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/1061244856034746)
  8. Complete the **Ad creative** section.
  9. In the **Destination** section, select **Website** and enter a website URL and display link.
**Note:** Starting in March 2026, all single media and Advantage+ catalog collection ads on Facebook Feed will no longer display a URL in the ad footer. 
  10. Add the call add-on:
     * If you selected the leads objective: In the **Browser add-ons** section, select **Call** and enter a phone number. Change your country code from the dropdown menu, if needed.
     * If you selected the sales objective: In the **Personalized destinations** section, click **Edit**. Select the **Browser add-ons** tab, turn on **Browser add-ons** and then select **Call** and enter a phone number. Change your country code from the dropdown menu, if needed. Click **Done**.
  11. In the **Tracking** section, click **Set up** to set up CRM events, website events and app events to track event datasets, if needed.
  12. When you're done, click **Publish** to publish your lead ad.


When your ad starts running, you can review [metrics for call ads](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/237108475737601).
When you create an ad with a call add-on, the call add-on component can only appear on your website when someone taps on the ad from Facebook Feed, Facebook Stories, Facebook Reels, Facebook Marketplace, Instagram feed and Instagram Stories. Your ad may still show in other placements, but it won’t include the call add-on component.
