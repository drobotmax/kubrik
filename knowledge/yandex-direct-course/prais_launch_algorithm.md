# Dmitry Prais - New Project Launch Algorithm (Yandex Direct)

Source: @prais_secret message #46 (2023-06-27)

## Step 0 - Metrica check
Check Metrica settings. Check goal triggers. For e-commerce - verify e-commerce tracking works.

## Step 1 - Master Campaigns (MK)
Create two MKs: one on keywords, one copy on automatic audience selection.
- Site needs basic SEO (h1, titles, descriptions) so Yandex understands content
- If MKs catch on - great, conversions incoming
- If not - also great: first 2-3 days (week) MKs "flood" impressions = collect stats (negatives + keywords for other campaign types)

## Step 2 - Product Master Campaign
Even for services, not just products. Build manual feed and launch.
- Usually on automatic audience selection
- Sometimes product MK catches when regular MKs don't

## Step 3 - Search Campaign
Volume depends on niche. Recently seeing well-performing accounts built on literally "5 keywords".
- Take main keywords, ask yourself: would I search for this service with this keyword?
- If 70-80% yes - take it
- Use negatives and keywords from Step 1
- First 1-2 weeks: clean search queries daily or every 3 days
- When trash reduces: expand with auto-targeting (target queries only)
- Set auto-targeting bid HIGHER than regular keyword bids
- Good auto-targeting keywords can be added to the campaign

## Step 4 - Product Gallery + Dynamic Search (e-commerce)
- Processing takes up to 3 days - normal
- Insert negatives from previous steps
- Only "Target" auto-targeting type (first in list)
- First 2-3 weeks: clean search queries every 3 days

## Step 5 - RSA (Yandex Display Network)
RSA is the "greatest random" - may or may not catch.
- Launch, spend 4-5x average CPA budget
- Go to stats > placements > disable junk
- Use pre-built stop-placement list from start

## Step 6a - Retargeting
Decide case by case.
- Note: retargeting is already ON by default in MKs
- For e-commerce: product MKs work as Smart Banners (= retargeting)
- Separate retargeting campaign risks breaking other campaigns

## Step 6b - MK conversion optimization
If MKs caught conversions (macro + micro goals):
- Try switching to pay-per-click on small budget (amount you can afford to lose)
- Watch 1-2 weeks, then decide

## Step 7 - Interest audiences
Set interest audiences to OBSERVATION mode across all campaign types.
- Over time, stats accumulate
- Small chance to clean junk traffic via audience adjustments

## Step 8 - Optimize
Turn off what doesn't work. Keep what works.
